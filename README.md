# HEIGHT Architectural Consultancy

HEIGHT is a global architectural consultancy specializing in structural integrity, vertical luxury, and sustainable engineering for the world's most iconic skylines.

## Digital Presence Website
A premium, editorial-style website featuring custom layouts, typography, and interactive components.

### Pages:
- **Home (`index.html`)**: Immersive cinematic presentation of our philosophy.
- **About (`about.html`)**: Details on our legacy, leadership, and process.
- **Services (`services.html`)**: Overview of structural engineering, building envelope design, and forensic architecture capabilities.
- **Projects (`projects.html`)**: Interactive showcase of structural masterpieces.
- **Insights (`insights.html`)**: The Weekly Monograph publication platform.
- **Contact (`contact.html`)**: Direct channel for project inquiries and studio locations.
